HISTORY

The SPCA movement originally started in England in 1824 and eventually opened its very first operations in South Africa in 1872. Following this, the first official animal hospital opened in 1920. The organisation continuously evolved throughout the 19th and 20th centuries to protect pets, wildlife, and farm animals, and today proudly offers full veterinary, rescue, and education services.


MISSION & VISION

Our core mission is to actively prevent cruelty and promote kindness towards animals through our various dedicated services. We strive daily to create a world where all animals are treated with respect and remain completely safe from harm.

OUR CORE FOCUS AREAS

We work strictly to remove animals from states of neglect and abuse while educating communities on compassionate animal care. We provide essential medical care and sterilization, uphold established animal welfare laws, and ensure animals are fed and live freely without pain or fear.
