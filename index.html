INTRODUCTION (Summary)

The SPCA (Society of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals) is a non-profit organisation that rescues, cares for, and protects animals. We dedicate our efforts to educating the public on responsible pet ownership, giving a vital second chance to animals in need, and ensuring they are provided with safe, permanent homes.

Domestic pets

We work closely with household domestic pets including cats and dogs.

Farm animals

Various wildlife species

We handle various wildlife species including bats, baboons, rabbits, raptors, porcupines, pigeons, seals, and snakes.
