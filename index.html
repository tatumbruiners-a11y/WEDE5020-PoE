The Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (SPCA)

The SPCA is a non-profit organisation dedicated to rescuing, caring and protecting animals. Our aim is to educate the public on responsible pet ownership, give a second chance to animals in need and provide them with safe homes.
